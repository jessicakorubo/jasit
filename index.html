Everything you need

One platform. Every school operation.

No more scattered spreadsheets, paper registers, or lost receipts. SmartLMS centralises everything your school runs on.

Student management

Register, track, and manage all your students — online and offline — with profiles, class assignments, and promotion history.

Fee management

Set fee structures, track payments, generate receipts, and identify defaulters — all without touching a spreadsheet.

CBT examination engine

Create and administer computer-based tests with automatic marking, instant results, and detailed performance analytics.

Result compilation

Compile and generate student report sheets automatically at the end of each term — no more manual calculations.

Attendance tracking

Mark and monitor daily attendance for every class. Get stats and trends at a glance without paper registers.

Teacher tools

Teachers get their own portal to upload schemes of work, lesson notes, submit assessments, and input student grades.
