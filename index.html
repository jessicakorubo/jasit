Everything you need

One school management platform. Every school operation.

No more scattered spreadsheets, paper registers, or lost fee receipts. SmartLMS centralises everything your Nigerian school runs on.

Student information management

Register, track, and manage all your students — online and offline — with profiles, class assignments, and complete academic history.

School fee management

Set fee categories, track partial payments per student, generate receipts, and know exactly who owes what — without touching a spreadsheet.

CBT examination engine

Create and administer computer-based tests with automatic marking, instant results, and detailed performance analytics. Prepares students for WAEC and NECO CBT.

Result compilation & report cards

Compile and generate student result sheets and report cards automatically at end of each term — no manual calculations, no errors.

Attendance tracking

Mark and monitor daily student and teacher attendance for every class. Get reports and trends at a glance — no paper registers needed.

Teacher & student portals

Teachers upload schemes of work, lesson notes, and submit grades. Students access their results, assignments, and CBT exams from their own dedicated portal.
